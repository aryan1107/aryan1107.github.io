About Imagen.

Imagen is a creative image tool for generating artwork from text, editing existing images, saving generated work, and building a personal style library. It is made for quick sketches, polished concepts, character ideas, product visuals, social posts, and any other image you want to explore.

Some tools run on your device. AI generation, editing, chat, and custom style training use online processing and may spend credits. The app includes a free starter tier, with optional paid plans for higher limits and more frequent use.

What's inside.

  • Text-to-image generation with aspect ratio, style, and model controls.
  • Image editing tools for refining or transforming existing pictures.
  • Assets and history so you can find what you created later.
  • Custom style training for looks you want to reuse.
  • A chat mode for iterating on image ideas conversationally.
