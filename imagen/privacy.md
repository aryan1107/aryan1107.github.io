# Privacy Policy — Imagen

This explains what information **Imagen** ("the app") handles, how it
is used, and what choices you have. Plain English where possible. If
anything is unclear, see the [support page](support.html) for ways to
reach us.

Imagen is offered with a free starter tier and optional paid plans. By
using Imagen you agree to the practices described here.

## Summary

Imagen lets you create, edit, save, and organize AI-generated images.
Some tools run on your device. Features that need AI processing,
custom style training, chat, syncing, purchase verification, or cloud
storage use trusted third-party services. We try to collect only what
is needed to run the app, protect abuse limits, process purchases, and
help you recover your work.

## What we collect

What we keep depends on how you use the app.

- **Account identity** — if you sign in, we store the account
  identifier and email address supplied by the sign-in provider. This
  lets you access your images, credits, custom styles, and purchase
  status across devices.
- **Anonymous or device reference** — if you use the app before
  signing in, the app may create an anonymous reference so credits,
  usage limits, support requests, and purchases can be associated with
  that install.
- **Prompts and generation settings** — text prompts, selected model,
  aspect ratio, style controls, and related settings are sent for AI
  processing when you create or edit an image.
- **Uploaded images** — images you attach for editing, style
  references, moodboards, or custom style training are uploaded only
  when you choose to use those features.
- **Generated images and assets** — images produced by the app may be
  stored so you can view them later in your assets, history, or chat.
- **Custom style data** — training images, style names, and generated
  model references are stored so your custom styles can be reused.
- **Usage and credit data** — credit balance, generation history,
  training counts, chat usage, and rate-limit information are stored
  so the app can enforce plan limits and prevent abuse.
- **Purchase and subscription receipts** — purchases are processed by
  the App Store. We never see your name, billing address, or card
  details. Purchase receipts are shared with a third-party payment and
  subscription service to validate access, restore purchases, and keep
  your plan status current.
- **Diagnostic and crash data** — if the app crashes or fails, device
  data such as app version, OS version, device model, error logs, and
  time of error may be sent to a diagnostic service to help fix bugs.
- **Support messages** — if you contact support, we keep your message
  and any details you include so we can reply and investigate the
  issue.

## What we don't collect

- **No payment card details.** Purchases are handled by the App Store.
- **No passwords.** Sign-in is handled by third-party sign-in systems.
- **No full photo library access.** When you pick an image, the app
  receives only the file or photo you chose.
- **No microphone or precise location.** Imagen does not need these to
  create images.
- **No selling.** We do not sell your personal data.

## AI processing

When you use AI features, your prompt, selected settings, and any
images you provide are sent to third-party AI processing services so
the requested image, edit, chat response, or custom style can be
created. Outputs may be stored by Imagen so you can reopen, download,
or continue editing them.

Please do not upload images or prompts you do not have the right to
use, or content that includes private information you do not want
processed by online services.

## Third-party services

A small number of trusted providers help run the app. We share only
what is needed for each service to work:

- **Authentication services** — to sign you in and keep your session
  active.
- **Cloud backend and storage** — to store account data, generated
  images, assets, custom styles, and usage records.
- **AI processing services** — to generate images, edit images, train
  custom styles, and answer chat requests.
- **Payment and subscription services** — to process purchases,
  verify receipts, restore access, and calculate plan status.
- **Diagnostic and crash reporting services** — to find and fix app
  crashes or server errors.
- **Analytics services** — to understand app usage in aggregate and
  improve the product.
- **Content safety services** — to help detect abuse or policy
  violations where needed.

Each provider has its own privacy policy and may process information
as part of providing its service.

## How long things are kept

- **Generated images and custom styles** stay available while your
  account exists, unless you delete them or ask us to delete them.
- **Uploaded training or editing inputs** may be kept as long as
  needed to provide the feature, debug failures, prevent abuse, or
  maintain your reusable style.
- **Usage and purchase records** may be kept as long as needed for
  billing, fraud prevention, support, accounting, or legal reasons.
- **Diagnostic and analytics data** is usually kept for a limited
  period by the relevant provider, then deleted or aggregated.
- **Support messages** are kept as long as needed to handle the issue
  and maintain a record of the conversation.

## Data deletion requests

If you want to delete your Imagen data, contact us through the
[support page](support.html) with the subject line **"Data Deletion
Request"**. If possible, include the email or user ID shown in the
app's account or support screen. We may need to keep limited purchase,
fraud-prevention, or legal records where required.

## Children

Imagen is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum
age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children under 13.

## Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures
to protect information processed through the app, including encryption
in transit. No method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute
security.

## Links to other sites

The app may contain links to external sites we do not operate. We are
not responsible for their content or privacy practices.
