# Support — Imagen

Need a hand? You're in the right place. There are two ways to reach
us: a one-tap **Support** button inside the app, or a regular email.

## The fastest way — in the app

Open Imagen, then go to **Settings → Contact Support** and tap the
**Support** button. It opens a pre-filled email that includes your
user reference, app version, device details, and plan information —
which means we can usually find and fix the problem on the first
reply, instead of going back-and-forth asking what device you're on.

If you can, please add:

- A short description of what you were trying to do.
- A screenshot or screen recording, if it's a visual issue.
- The prompt or image workflow involved, if the issue is about
  generation, editing, chat, or training.

## Common questions

### How do I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by the App Store. Manage or cancel in
**App Store → Apple ID → Subscriptions** on iOS, or **System Settings
→ Apple ID → Media & Purchases → Manage** on Mac. Cancellations take
effect at the end of the current billing period.

### How do I restore my purchase?

Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase, then in the app
go to **Settings → Restore Purchases**. Your active plan should
reactivate after the App Store confirms it.

### Why did a generation fail but credits changed?

Most failed generations release pending credits automatically. If
your balance still looks wrong after reopening the app, contact us
through the in-app **Support** button so the email includes the user
reference needed to investigate.

### Where are my generated images?

Open **Assets** in the app. Images created while signed in should
appear there for quick access. If something is missing, check that
you're signed into the same account and contact support with the
approximate time it was created.

### How do I delete my data?

Contact us through the in-app **Support** button or email
[app+imagen \[at\] brutusai \[dot\] com](mailto:app+imagen@brutusai.com)
with the subject line **Data Deletion Request**. Include the user ID
shown in the app if possible.

### Can I use generated images commercially?

You are responsible for reviewing generated images and making sure
your use is allowed in your situation. AI outputs can be similar to
other outputs and may contain mistakes or unexpected details, so use
care for public or commercial work.

### Something else?

Tap the **Support** button in the app, or email
[app+imagen \[at\] brutusai \[dot\] com](mailto:app+imagen@brutusai.com).
We read every message.
