Depression

Depression

Common Signs and Symptoms of Depression
  • A feeling of Helplessness/hopelessness
  • Lack of enjoyment or pleasure in work or any other activities that used to be enjoyable before
  • Constant feeling of extreme sadness.
  • A sense of worthlessness.
  • Having difficulty in concentration, lack of energy and tiredness.
  • Having difficulties with food and sleep habits.
