Substance Abuse

Substance Abuse


Common Signs and Symptoms of Substance Abuse

  • Experiencing unusual instances of hyperactivity.
  • Mood swings and paranoia.
  • Tremors.
  • Change in behaviour in terms of eating, sleeping.
  • Loss of interests in hobbies, sports or other activities.
