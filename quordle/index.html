About Quordle.

Quordle is a daily word puzzle for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro. You have nine guesses to solve four five-letter words at the same time — easier said than done. Every day brings a fresh puzzle; the practice mode is unlimited.

The free version is supported by ads. A one-time Premium upgrade removes them and unlocks a few small extras. Either way, your streak and stats sync across your Apple devices, and home-screen and lock-screen widgets keep today's status one glance away.

What's inside.

  • A new four-word daily puzzle, plus unlimited practice.
  • Home-screen and lock-screen widgets for streaks at a glance.
  • Siri shortcuts and Spotlight search — say it or search it, jump straight in.
  • A gentle reminder if you've been away for a day or more, never spammy.
  • iCloud sync so your stats follow you between devices.
  • Built-in chat helper to talk through tricky boards (optional).
