# Privacy Policy — Quordle

This explains what information **Quordle** ("the app") handles, how it
is used, and what choices you have. Plain English where possible. If
anything is unclear, see the [support page](support.html) for ways to
reach us.

Quordle is offered free of charge with optional in-app purchases. By
using Quordle you agree to the practices described here.

## Summary

Quordle is **anonymous** — no sign-up, no username, no email. We do
not create or store an account for you. Your puzzle progress lives on
your device. The only data we share with third parties is a **device
identifier (Device ID)** — used for app analytics, advertising in the
free version, and app functionality. You can disable personalized
advertising on iOS at any time.

## What we collect

We try to collect as little as possible.

- **On-device game data** — daily and practice puzzle progress,
  streaks, win/loss history, preferences (theme, sound, haptics,
  reminder time), and onboarding state. This stays on your device.
  We do not upload it to a server tied to your identity.
- **Device ID (identifier)** — a non-personal identifier from your
  device used to:
  - measure aggregate app analytics (which features get used),
  - serve advertising in the free version (subject to your iOS
    tracking choice — see ATT below),
  - support core app functionality (e.g., associating a purchase
    with the device that bought it).
  This is the data type listed under "Identifiers" in the App Store
  privacy nutrition label.
- **Purchase & subscription receipts** — purchases are processed by
  the App Store. We never see your name, billing address, or card
  details. A purchase receipt is shared with our subscription
  service to validate the purchase, restore it on other devices, and
  check whether Premium is active. The receipt is keyed to your
  Apple ID, not to any account we maintain.
- **Diagnostic & crash data** — if the app crashes, anonymous device
  data (model, OS version, app version, error stack trace, time of
  error) is sent to a third-party diagnostic tool to help us fix
  bugs. No personal data is included.

## What we don't collect

- **No accounts.** We do not create accounts. There is no username,
  no email, no password.
- **No personal contact info.** We don't ask for, collect, or store
  your name, email, phone number, or address.
- **No chat history.** Quordle has an optional in-app chat helper.
  Your messages are sent to a third-party AI service to generate a
  response and are **not retained by us**. We don't keep a
  server-side copy of your conversations. (The third-party AI
  service has its own privacy policy that governs whatever it does
  with the prompt while it's processing it.)
- **No selling.** We don't sell your data. Ever.
- **No iCloud / photos / contacts.** We don't read anything outside
  the app.

## Advertising and App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

The free version of Quordle is supported by ads. To keep the app
free and ads relevant, our ad partners may use your device's
advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS).

On iOS, Apple requires apps to ask before any cross-app tracking.
The first time you set up Quordle (or shortly after, if you already
had it installed before ads shipped), you'll see Apple's standard
**App Tracking Transparency** prompt:

- **Allow tracking** — ads may be personalized to your interests.
- **Ask not to track** — no advertising identifier is shared. Ads
  still appear, but won't be personalized.

The app works identically either way. There is no penalty, locked
content, or feature loss based on your tracking choice.

You can change this at any time:

- **iOS-wide:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
- **Per app:** Settings → Quordle → Allow Tracking

If you upgrade to Premium, ads are removed entirely and no
ad-related identifiers are shared.

## Third-party services

A small number of trusted providers help run the app. We share with
them only the information they need to do their job, and they are
bound not to use it for anything else:

- **Payment & subscription processing** — to validate purchases,
  restore them on other devices, and check Premium status.
- **Advertising services** — to display ads in the free version and
  report aggregate performance.
- **Diagnostic & crash reporting** — to detect and fix bugs.
- **Analytics** — to understand, in aggregate, how the app is used.
- **AI chat** — the optional in-app chat helper sends your messages
  to an AI service for processing. Messages are not retained by us.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and may collect
information as part of providing its service.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications during onboarding, the app may schedule a
single gentle reminder if you've been away for a day or more. These
reminders are scheduled and delivered locally by your device —
nothing is sent from a server, and we do not track whether you
opened them.

You can turn them off at any time in **Settings → Notifications →
Quordle**.

## Photo Library

If you tap "Save Image" on a result screen, the app asks permission
to save the image to your Photo Library. The image is saved locally
on your device. We do not read or upload anything from your library.

## How long things are kept

- **Local game data** — stays on your device until you delete the
  app. Removing the app erases everything.
- **Purchase records** — may be kept by the App Store and our
  subscription service as long as required for accounting or legal
  reasons.
- **Diagnostic & crash data** — typically kept for a limited period
  by the diagnostic provider, then deleted.
- **Aggregate analytics** — non-identifying, may be kept indefinitely.

Because we do not maintain an account for you, there is nothing on
our side to "delete" — uninstalling the app removes your data on
your device. You can also reset your advertising identifier in iOS
Settings any time.

## Children

in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children under 13.
