About Worldle.

Worldle is a daily geography puzzle for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Each day we show the silhouette of a country — your job is to guess which one. Wrong guesses give you a distance, direction, and proximity hint to nudge you toward the answer. Six guesses, one country, fresh every 24 hours.

A second daily round, Daily Flags, lives one tap away. Free to play with ads; a one-time Premium upgrade removes them and unlocks a few small extras. Streaks and stats live on your device — no account needed.

What's inside.

  • A new daily geography puzzle, plus Daily Flags as a bonus round.
  • Home-screen and lock-screen widgets that show your streak at a glance.
  • Siri shortcuts and Spotlight search — say it or search it, jump straight in.
  • A gentle reminder if you've been away for a day or more, never spammy.
  • An in-app AI helper for hints and quick geography questions.
