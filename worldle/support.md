# Support — Worldle

Need a hand? You're in the right place. There are two ways to reach
us: a one-tap **Support** button inside the app, or a regular email.

## The fastest way — in the app

Open Worldle, then go to **Settings → Contact Support** and tap the
**Support** button. It opens a pre-filled email that includes an
anonymous device reference, the app version, and your device details
— which means we can usually find and fix the problem on the first
reply, instead of going back-and-forth asking what device you're on.

If you can, please add:

- A short description of what you were trying to do.
- A screenshot or screen recording, if it's a visual issue.

## Common questions

### How do I cancel Premium?

Premium is an App Store subscription. Manage or cancel it in **App
Store → Apple ID → Subscriptions** on iOS, or **System Settings →
Apple ID → Media & Purchases → Manage** on Mac. Cancellations take
effect at the end of the current billing period.

### How do I restore Premium on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase, then in the
app go to **Settings → Restore Purchases**. Premium will reactivate.

### How do I delete my data?

Worldle is anonymous — there is no account to delete. All your game
data lives on your device. To remove everything, just delete the
app from your device and your data is gone.

### How do I turn off notifications?

**Settings (iOS) → Notifications → Worldle**, then toggle off. Or
inside the app: **Settings → Notifications**.

### How do I change my tracking choice?

**Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking** (iOS-wide), or
**Settings → Worldle → Allow Tracking** (per-app).

### My streak / stats look wrong

Your streak is recorded once per local calendar day when you open
the app. If a day looks missing, make sure your device's date and
time are set automatically (**Settings → General → Date & Time →
Set Automatically**). If it still looks off, reach out via the
in-app **Support** button — it auto-attaches the anonymous
reference we need to debug.

### Daily Flags vs the main Daily puzzle

These are two separate daily rounds. Each has its own streak in
the web game, but the home-screen widget shows a single combined
"open streak" — one tick per day you launch the app, regardless of
which game(s) you played.
